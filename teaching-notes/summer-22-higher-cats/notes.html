This is a reading group following Denis-Charles Cisinski's book ``Higher Categories and Homotopical Algebra''. Our goals are to learn the theory of ∞-categories (namely incarnated as quasi-categories) with an eye towards higher algebra.

We are meeting Saturdays at 16:30 UTC (09:30 PDT). Our first meeting was Saturday July 30, 2022. There is a discord server. Please email me if you would like the discord/zoom links.

RESOURCES:

  • ``Higher Categories and Homotopical Algebra'' -- Cisinski
  • Kerodon
  • ``A short course on ∞-categories'' -- Groth
  • ``On ∞-topoi'' -- Lurie (``the original HTT'')
  • ``An Invitation to Higher Algebra'' -- Mazel-Gee
  • ``Introduction to ∞-Categories'' -- Haugseng
  • ``Introduction to Quasicategories'' -- Rezk
