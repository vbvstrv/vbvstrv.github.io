This is a reading group following Denis-Charles Cisinski's book ``Higher Categories and Homotopical Algebra''. Our goals are to learn the theory of ∞-categories (namely incarnated as quasi-categories) with an eye towards higher algebra.

We are meeting Saturdays at 16:30 UTC (09:30 PDT). Our first meeting was Saturday July 30, 2022. There is a discord server. Please email me if you would like the discord/zoom links.

RESOURCES:

  • "Higher Categories and Homotopical Algebra" -- Denis-Charles Cisinski
  • Kerodon -- Jacob Lurie
  • "Higher Topos Theory" -- Jacob Lurie
  • "A short course on ∞-categories'' -- Moritz Groth/Rahn
  • "Quasi-categories and Kan complexes" -- André Joyal
  • "An Invitation to Higher Algebra'' -- Aaron Mazel-Gee
  • "Introduction to ∞-Categories'' -- Rune Haugseng
  • "Introduction to Quasicategories'' -- Charles Rezk
  • "Elements of ∞-Category Theory" -- Emily Riehl & Dominic Verity
  • "Calculus of fractions and homotopy theory" -- Pierre Gabriel and Michel Zisman
